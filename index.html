About Curie AI Tools

How does Curie know what each payer requires?

We maintain a curated database of 500+ insurer policy PDFs, extracted and structured using AI. The system automatically maps clinical criteria, documentation requirements, and formulary rules for each payer and medication combination — updated monthly.

Is Curie's prior authorization letter generator free?

Yes. Curie AI tools are completely free for all verified US healthcare professionals. No subscription fee, no usage limit, no patient data stored. Sign in with your NPI number to access all clinical AI tools.
